About The App

Competitive programming (CP) a large growing community is very well known for algorithms, data structures and Mathematics, and is a must have skill for any computer science student or top tech companies job aspirant.

CrazyCoder is born out of our need to have a single place to view all the coding contests happening across many platforms. The app automatically updates all the coding contests and hackathons. You will never miss any contest. CrazyCoder aims to promote and help grow the competitive programming community worldwide.

CrazyCoder is a comprehensive app designed to cater to the needs of the competitive programming community. It serves as a one-stop solution for viewing coding contests and hackathons happening across multiple platforms. With its user-friendly interface and automatic updates, CrazyCoder ensures that you never miss any contest, enabling you to stay on top of your competitive programming journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Crazy Coder?

Crazy Coder is a mobile app designed espicially for Competitive Programming(CP).

2. How does Crazy Coder benefit Competitive Programmers?

Crazy Coder offers a large variety of features such as - Contest Tracking, Reminders, Leaderboard, SDE Interview Section, Progress Tracking etc.

3. What features does Crazy Coder offer?

Crazy Coder offers a range of state-of-the-art features to enhance the creative process. Some of the notable features include:

- Contest Tracking: See contests platform-wise and easily differentiate between running and upcoming contests.

- Reminder: Set reminders for contests to receive notifications before they start, so you never miss an important competition.

- Leaderboard: Compare your rank with friends and engage in healthy competition to motivate each other.

- SDE Interview Section: Access a dedicated section for interview preparation, recommended by employees of top tech companies (MAANG companies).

- Chat With Friends: Connect with fellow programmers, discuss coding problems, and share your progress.

- Progress Tracking: Keep track of your own progress and see how you are improving over time.

- Profile Page: Visit your profile page directly from the app to manage your personal information and preferences.

4. How can I download Crazy Coder?

To download Crazy Coder, simply visit the respective app store for your mobile device and search for "Crazy Coder." Follow the installation instructions to get started and begin your competitive programming journey with CrazyCoder.

5. Is Crazy Coder available for both iOS and Android devices?

No, Crazy Coder is designed to be compatible only with Android platforms. You can download and use Crazy Coder on your Android smartphone or tablet.

6. Is Crazy Coder a free app?

Crazy Coder is a free app with essential features to kickstart competitive programmers creative journey.

7. Is Crazy Coder available as an open-source project?

Yes, Crazy Coder is available as an open-source project.
